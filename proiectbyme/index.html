Our locations

Paradise Cove

Surrounded by tropical gardens, the bluest lagoons, rugged coastlines, and majestic mountain ranges, Paradise Cove offers unique and distinct locations for your special day. Each corner of paradise reflects not only the beauty of Hawaii but its everlasting culture and traditions. Located against the ocean lagoon at Paradise Cove, the Beach Gardens enchant its visitors with a stunning view of the Pacific Ocean and the mountains along the Waianae Coastline. Fragrant flowers and lush evergreen fauna envelop all in attendance at the Ocean Gardens, radiating a tropical vibe found nowhere else but in Hawaii. For a ceremony right on the beach, let your friends and family dig their toes into the white sand at as they watch on as you exchange your vows.


Ocean Garden at Ko Olina Oahu

Experience all of what Hawaii has to offer with sparkling blue ocean front, the beauty of sweeping lawns and fragrant tropical gardens, and a festive lu’au under the stars. Hawaii always makes you feel at ease but the Ocean Garden at Ko Olina Oahu takes it to another level. A wedding at the Ocean Garden makes the most of the island’s breathtaking natural beauty so no two weddings will ever be the same. After the ceremony, you can enjoy an intimate reception in one of many other unique locations on the property, or take part in a lu’au with live music, games, authentic food and Hawaiian dancers.


Gannon’s Restaurant

Located on the Wailea Gold and Emerald Golf Course, the panoramic views from Gannon’s Restaurant spans from the mountain side of Haleakala to the deep blue waters of the Pacific Ocean. The Lower Knoll is the perfect backdrop for your stunning outdoor ceremony. Located in front of the restaurant grounds, this special setting features bright bougainvillea, palm trees, and a spectacular view. Or say your vows at the summit of the golf course at the secluded Molokini Lookout. You and your guests will adore the commanding views and secluded locale. Surrounded by lush tropical beauty at every turn, a wedding day at Gannon’s ensures that your extraordinary day is everything that you’ve imagined.
